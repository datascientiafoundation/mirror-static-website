Tshwaraganyo go ya ka go farologana ga rona
\nLekwalo la go itlhalosa<\/strong><\/h3>\n
    \n
  1. Tshedimosetso\u00a0<\/i>e leng teng mo inthanete, e dira gore go nne le tokafalo ya botlhale e ntsha mo bathong e re ratang go e bula go le gontsi, go akareditswe le kgonagats le botoka mo boleng jwa botshelo.<\/li>\n
  2. Tshedimosetso\u00a0e tshwanetse go nna teng le go dirisiwa ka tlhatlhwa e e ka kgonang ke batho botlhe kwa ntle ga morafe,bong, tumelo, morafe,le selekanyego sa khumo kgotsa dintlha dingwe tse di ikaegileng.<\/li>\n
  3. Tshedimosetso\u00a0 e tshwanetse go emela lefatshe sentle ka go farologana le go lemoga, ka go baka ke sesto le dingwao tsa lefelo leo, tlhokoge le lobaka. Tshedimosetso e tshwanetse go tlhalosiwa ka dipuong\u00a0tse di buiwang mo\u00a0mafelong a batho le go gatisa dintlha tsa nnete tse di tshwanetsego, ka tsela le mekgwa e e tshwanetseng thata.<\/li>\n
  4. Kitso e tshwanetse go monya tshedimosetso\u00a0e e farologaneng teng go gatela pele thata kutlwano ditshwantsho tsa kutlwano\/seoposengwe ka fag are ga yone.<\/li>\n
  5. Kitso le tshedimosetso\u00a0tshwanetse go tlhabologa ditebogo go matsapa a boithaopo ba ditlhopa tse di naleng kgatlhego, e mongwe le mongwe a e dirang ka boipuso jo bo tletseng .<\/li>\n
  6. DataScientia \u00a0ke mokgatllho yoo se nang le (yo o tlhokang) poelo,yo boikaelelo ba ona e leng go tlhabolola dilo tsa motheo \u00a0go rotloetsa go tlhaga ga seoposengwe se se nang le kitso, ka go emelwa ka tshedimosetso.<\/li>\n
  7. Boikaelelo ba ntlha ba DataScientia ke dilo tsa motheo tsa botegeniki\/ thekenoloji<\/li>\n
  8. Boikaelelo ba bobedi ba DataScientia ke dilo tsa motheo tsa mokgatllho ka tlhabololo le tiriso ya karolo ya mekgwa e botoka.<\/li>\n
  9. Boikaelelo ba boraro ba DataScientia ke dilo tsa motheo tsa semolao, tirisano, Gareng ga\u00a0tse\u00a0dingwe, dikgang tse di amang tetleletso le bonosi. \u00a0<\/li>\n
  10. Boikaelelo ba bone ba DataScientia ke dilo tsa motheo tsa set\u0161haba, e leng set\u0161haba se\u00a0se ikaegileng go tlhaloganya tshedimosetso, tlhabologo le tiriso ya ditlamelo tsa yone, mo go set\u0161haba le itsholelo.<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n
