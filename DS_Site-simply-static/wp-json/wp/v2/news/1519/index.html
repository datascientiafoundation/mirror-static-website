The\u00a0workshop<\/span>\u00a0aims to draw the attention of researchers in the under resourced languages\u00a0 communities and encourage them to cooperate and intensify efforts to provide solutions and\u00a0 resources for such under resourced languages. To achieve this goal, the\u00a0workshop<\/span>\u00a0offers the possibility for researchers to organize competitions for solving NLP tasks. These tasks are usually provided with training and test data by the task organizers to allow participating solutions to be evaluated and compared with other solutions. The number of shared tasks for this year are 8 tasks for 5 languages (http:\/\/nsurl.org\/tasks\/<\/a>).\u00a0 Task description papers and the participating solutions including the performance evaluations will be published in the\u00a0workshop<\/span>proceedings. In addition, we invite submissions on topics that include but are not limited to\u00a0 the following:<\/p>\n

Low level NLP solutions<\/b>:
\nPOS-tagging
\nTokenization
\nNamed entity\u00a0 recognition
\nMorphological analysis
\nChunking
\nDisambiguation<\/p>\n

Resources for low level NLP<\/b>:<\/p>\n

Dictionaries
\nAnnotated data
\nCorpora<\/p>\n

Important dates<\/b>:
\nSubmission deadline<\/b>: 30 June 2019
\nNotification of acceptance<\/b>: 22 July 2019
\nCamera-ready paper due<\/b>: 12 August 2019
\nWorkshop<\/span>\u00a0dates<\/b>: 11,12 September 2019
