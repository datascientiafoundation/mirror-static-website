Innovation Services

Create Solutions. Enable Startups. Build a People-Centric Future.

Innovation at DataScientia means more than invention—it means creating solutions that matter. We help transform research and education into real-world applications, services, and startups that prioritize human values. Our innovation ecosystem connects contributors, developers, and communities to build responsible AI tools and data services for a better society.

Data That Drives Innovation

Our data sharing ecosystem is built on real-life projects across diverse communities, resulting in culturally rich and high-quality datasets. These datasets empower innovators to develop ethical, inclusive, and impactful AI solutions grounded in real-world diversity.

Our Data Catalogs

Fuel Innovation with Open Data Sharing. Explore our data catalogs to find data of high quality that fits your needs and innovation opportunities across communities.

Explore DataScientia Marketplace

LiveData Catalogs

Centralizes access to domain-specific community data, enabling seamless exploration and integration across diverse data types. Fuels innovation across sectors like health, education, and sustainability. Explore our LiveData Catalog.

LivePeople Catalog

Provides access to ethically collected datasets on human behavior, lifestyle, and habits—crucial for developing people-centric, adaptive technologies. Data is protected and available under license to ensure responsible use. Explore our LivePeople Catalog. 

LiveLanguage Catalog

Enables the development of multilingual and culturally-aware AI by offering rich, open-access datasets on cross-lingual semantics. Ideal for innovations in translation, language learning, and global communication tools. Explore our LiveLanguage Catalog.

LiveKnowledge Catalog

Supports intelligent system design by providing structured knowledge resources such as ontologies and schemas. These resources help power semantic technologies, reasoning engines, and context-aware AI. Explore our LiveKnowledge Catalog.

Partner with Us for Innovation

Institutions, universities, civic organizations, and ecosystem builders are all welcome to join our innovation network.
Partnership with DataScientia ensures transparency, traceability, and ethical data use—unlocking opportunities to co-create services not only for your own goals, but also for the wider community and fellow partners. Together, we turn shared knowledge into shared impact.

Partner with DataScientia to:

  • Co-develop Ethical AI Services

  • Launch Living Labs to test ideas in real-world settings

  • Support Start-ups & Spin-offs

  • Share access to data, tools, and shared research spaces
