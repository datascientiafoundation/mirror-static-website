\n\t\tWhat is DataScientia?\n\t<\/h3>\n\t

DataScientia is a Citizen Science community if people who are interesteed in learning about contributing to the data driven Artificial Intelligence (AI) technological evolution, toward a better world, for themselves, for society, and for the world.<\/p>\n

\n\t\tWhat do we do?\n\t<\/h3>\n\t

The main goal of DataScentia is to generate and share knowledge about people, society, and the world, as seen and described by people. Our ultimate goal is to analyze and understand the diversity of people, e.g., how location and time, language, culture,competences, skills, opinions and goals…<\/p>\n

\n\t\tWhy do we do what do we do?\n\t<\/h3>\n\t

We all live in our own world which is different from that of everybody else. Perception creates it, language allows to share its description with the other people, thus causing its objectivation, knowledge is what we come to learn about it by observing what repeats itself through change.<\/p>\n

\n\t\tWhat do we hope to achive?\n\t<\/h3>\n\t

We want to enable a process where people increase their level of awareness and active participation to the current AI technological innovation.<\/p>\n

\n\t\tWho are the members of our community?\n\t<\/h3>\n\t

Anybody can be a member of the Community. You do not need any specific skills to become a member. The only requirement is that you must be willing to learn about AI and its impact on society and to invest some effort in this<\/p>\n

\n\t\tHow can you contribute?\n\t<\/h3>\n\t

There are at least three types of contribution. You can choose the one which best fits your competence, skills and interests:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • You can provide data as needed in one of the many experiments that we set up<\/li>\n
  • Participate to the open-source community, in charge of development of the AI tools and software that allows DataScientia to operate.<\/li>\n
  • Help us in the management of one of the DataScientia local communities<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    \n\t\tHow do we help you contribute?\n\t<\/h3>\n\t

    There are four main ways we can help you.<\/p>\n

      \n
    • We provide with educational, mainly online, material which will provide the basics of AI and its impact on society<\/li>\n
    • We will help you in learning how to collect, store and manage the person-centric data that you generate<\/li>\n
    • We will help you to use AI towards learning how to understand person-centric data<\/li>\n
    • We will teach you how to use the DataScientia tools and software for data collection, understanding and also exploitation<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\t\t\t\t\n\t

      \n\t\tHow are we organized?\n\t<\/h3>\n\t

      DataScientia is a non-profit organization working in strict collaboration with its partner Universities. Universities support DataScientia in its lifelong educational program as well as in its research and innovation projects.<\/p>\n

      DataScientia is a glocal organization with many local nodes consisting in the universities and communities they support, and with one global node coordinating the activities of the local nodes.<\/p>\n

      The global node provides the supporting infrastructure, as needed (see the manifesto). The global node acts under the strategic guidelines provided by the local nodes.<\/p>\n

      \n\t\tHow do we sustain ourself?\n\t<\/h3>\n\t

      We provide services enabled by the data and technologies developed by DataScientia and its partner universities. Those are organized around five main types of services:<\/p>\n

        \n
      1. We collect data<\/li>\n
      2. We store and manage data<\/li>\n
      3. We enable finding data<\/li>\n
      4. We distribute data<\/li>\n
      5. We enable the purpose-driven composition of data by third parts<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

        Each of the six types of data will enable different data-specific servicies.<\/p>\n\t

        The DataScientia Manifesto<\/center><\/h2>\n
        \n

        \n\t\tUnity through diversity\n\t<\/h3>\n\t

        <\/h3>\n
          \n
        1. Data, as they are available in the Internet, are the fuel that is boosting the rise of a new smarter society that we want more open, inclusive and enabling a better quality of life.<\/li>\n
        2. Data should be available and reusable at affordable costs by everybody, independently of race, sex, religion, nationality, level of wealth or any other contingent factor.<\/li>\n
        3. Data should properly represent the diversity of the worlds that are perceived, as they result from the local culture and traditions, needs and purpose. Data should be expressed in the local languages and should record those facts that are contextually relevant, with the format and values which are most appropriate.<\/li>\n
        4. Knowledge should absorb the diversity of data into progressively more unified representations of the unity underlying it.<\/li>\n
        5. Knowledge and data should be developed thanks to the voluntary efforts of the interested parties, each operating in full autonomy.<\/li>\n
        6. DataScientia is a not-for-profit organization whose aim is the development of an infrastructure supporting the emergence of a unitary knowledge-driven understanding of diversity, as represented by data.<\/li>\n
        7. DataScientia’s first aim is the technological infrastructure.<\/li>\n
        8. DataScientia’s second aim is the organizational infrastructure, via the development and adoption of shared best practices.<\/li>\n
        9. DataScientia’s third aim is the legal infrastructure, dealing, among others, with the issues related to licensing and privacy.<\/li>\n
        10. DataScientia’s fourth aim is the social infrastructure, that is, a community centered around the understanding of data, their development and their exploitation, both social and economic.<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n\n\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
